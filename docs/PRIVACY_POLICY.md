## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Infrastructure and Storage

Your data is stored using industry-leading cloud infrastructure:

- **Database:** PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase, which runs on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure
- **Authentication:** Secure authentication services provided by Supabase Auth
- **File Storage:** Secure cloud storage through Supabase Storage (AWS S3)
- **Data Centers:** Your data is stored in secure data centers located in the United States

### 3.2 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption of data in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
- Encryption of data at rest using AES-256 encryption
- Secure token-based authentication
- Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access authorized data
- Regular security updates and patches

**Operational Safeguards:**
- Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
- Regular security audits and assessments
- Incident response procedures
- Employee security training

**Application Security:**
- Secure credential storage using platform-specific secure storage (iOS Keychain, Android Keystore)
- Session management and automatic timeout
- Input validation and sanitization

### 3.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you and any applicable regulatory authorities as required by law, within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

## 8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

### 8.1 Children's Information

Love2Learn is a tutoring management platform designed for tutors to manage their tutoring business. While the Service stores information about students, including children under the age of 13, this information is provided by tutors and parents, not directly by children.

### 8.2 Parental Consent

**Tutors' Responsibility:** Tutors using the Service are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from parents or legal guardians before entering any student information into the system. By entering student information, tutors represent and warrant that they have obtained the necessary parental consent.

**Parents' Consent:** When parents create accounts, they consent to the collection and use of their children's information as described in this Privacy Policy.

### 8.3 What We Do Not Collect from Children

- We do not allow children under 13 to create accounts
- We do not collect personal information directly from children
- We do not target advertising to children

### 8.4 Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:
- Review their child's information stored in the Service
- Request deletion of their child's information
- Refuse further collection of their child's information

To exercise these rights, parents should contact their tutor directly or email us at privacy@love2learn.app.

### 8.5 Student Data Protection

We are committed to protecting student data and comply with applicable student data privacy laws, including:
- The Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), where applicable
- State student data privacy laws
