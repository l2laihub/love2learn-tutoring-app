## iOS App Store Assets

### Required Assets Checklist

#### App Icon
- [ ] **App Store Icon**: 1024 x 1024 px (PNG, no alpha/transparency)
  - No rounded corners (Apple applies them automatically)
  - No words or small details that won't be visible at small sizes
  - Must match the app icon design

#### Screenshots (Required for each supported device)

| Device Type | Dimensions | Minimum | Maximum |
|-------------|------------|---------|---------|
| iPhone 6.9" (15 Pro Max) | 1320 x 2868 px | 3 | 10 |
| iPhone 6.7" (14 Pro Max) | 1290 x 2796 px | 3 | 10 |
| iPhone 6.5" (11 Pro Max) | 1284 x 2778 px | 3 | 10 |
| iPhone 5.5" (8 Plus) | 1242 x 2208 px | 3 | 10 |
| iPad Pro 12.9" (6th gen) | 2048 x 2732 px | 3 | 10 |
| iPad Pro 12.9" (2nd gen) | 2048 x 2732 px | 3 | 10 |

**Note:** You can use 6.7" screenshots to autofill 6.5" if similar aspect ratio. Same for iPad generations.

#### App Preview Videos (Optional but Recommended)
- [ ] **iPhone**: 886 x 1920 px or 1080 x 1920 px (15-30 seconds)
- [ ] **iPad**: 1200 x 1600 px (15-30 seconds)
- Format: H.264, 30fps recommended
- No letterboxing

#### Text Assets
- [ ] **App Name**: Max 30 characters
- [ ] **Subtitle**: Max 30 characters
- [ ] **Promotional Text**: Max 170 characters (can be updated without review)
- [ ] **Description**: Max 4000 characters
- [ ] **Keywords**: Max 100 characters (comma-separated)
- [ ] **What's New**: Max 4000 characters (for updates)
- [ ] **Support URL**: Required
- [ ] **Privacy Policy URL**: Required
- [ ] **Marketing URL**: Optional

#### Category Selection
- **Primary**: Education
- **Secondary**: Business (recommended for tutor tools)

## Google Play Store Assets

### Required Assets Checklist

#### App Icon
- [ ] **Hi-res Icon**: 512 x 512 px (PNG, 32-bit with alpha)

#### Feature Graphic (Required)
- [ ] **Feature Graphic**: 1024 x 500 px (PNG or JPEG)
  - Displayed at the top of your store listing
  - Should convey app purpose at a glance
  - Avoid text that may be cut off at edges

#### Screenshots

| Device Type | Aspect Ratio | Minimum | Maximum | Dimensions |
|-------------|--------------|---------|---------|------------|
| Phone | 16:9 or 9:16 | 2 | 8 | Min 320px, Max 3840px |
| 7" Tablet | 16:9 or 9:16 | 0 | 8 | Min 320px, Max 3840px |
| 10" Tablet | 16:9 or 9:16 | 0 | 8 | Min 320px, Max 3840px |

**Recommended Phone Screenshot Sizes:**
- Portrait: 1080 x 1920 px or 1440 x 2560 px
- Landscape: 1920 x 1080 px or 2560 x 1440 px

#### Promotional Video (Optional)
- [ ] **YouTube Video**: Link to promotional video
  - Should be 30 seconds to 2 minutes
  - Landscape format preferred

#### Text Assets
- [ ] **App Name**: Max 30 characters
- [ ] **Short Description**: Max 80 characters
- [ ] **Full Description**: Max 4000 characters
- [ ] **Developer Name**: Your name or company
- [ ] **Developer Email**: Required
- [ ] **Privacy Policy URL**: Required
- [ ] **Developer Website**: Recommended

#### Additional Requirements
- [ ] **Content Rating Questionnaire**: Complete the IARC questionnaire
- [ ] **Data Safety Form**: Declare data collection practices
- [ ] **Target Audience**: Select appropriate age groups
- [ ] **App Category**: Education > Education

## App Icon Specifications

### Design Requirements

| Platform | Size | Format | Notes |
|----------|------|--------|-------|
| iOS App Store | 1024 x 1024 px | PNG (no alpha) | Square, Apple adds corners |
| iOS App Icon | Multiple sizes | PNG | Provided via asset catalog |
| Android Play Store | 512 x 512 px | PNG (32-bit) | Can have transparency |
| Android Adaptive | Foreground + Background | PNG | 108 x 108 dp safe zone |

### Current Love2Learn Icon Assets

Located in `/assets/`:
- `icon.png` - Main app icon (1024 x 1024 px recommended)
- `adaptive-icon.png` - Android adaptive icon foreground
- `favicon.png` - Web favicon
- `splash.png` - Splash screen

### Icon Design Best Practices
1. Keep design simple and recognizable at small sizes
2. Use the Love2Learn brand colors
3. Avoid photographs or complex gradients
4. Test icon at multiple sizes (29px, 60px, 180px, 1024px)
5. Ensure sufficient contrast

## Screenshot Guidelines

### Recommended Screenshot Content (In Order)

1. **Hero Screenshot**: Dashboard/main screen showing key value proposition
2. **Session Management**: Calendar or session scheduling view
3. **Student Progress**: Progress tracking or reports
4. **Invoicing**: Payment management or invoice generation
5. **Parent Portal**: Parent view of student progress
6. **Additional Features**: Materials sharing, messaging, etc.

### Screenshot Design Tips

1. **Use Device Frames**: Show screenshots in device mockups
2. **Add Captions**: Brief text explaining each feature (top or bottom)
3. **Consistent Style**: Same fonts, colors, background across all screenshots
4. **Show Real Data**: Use realistic (but fake) student names and data
5. **Highlight Key Features**: Use arrows or highlights for important elements

### Screenshot Caption Suggestions

| Screenshot | Caption |
|------------|---------|
| Dashboard | "Your tutoring business at a glance" |
| Sessions | "Schedule lessons in seconds" |
| Students | "Track every student's progress" |
| Invoicing | "Get paid faster with automatic invoicing" |
| Parent View | "Keep parents in the loop" |
| Materials | "Share resources effortlessly" |

### Tools for Creating Screenshots

- **Figma** (Free): Professional design tool with templates
- **Screenshots.pro**: Automated screenshot generator
- **AppMockUp**: Quick device frame mockups
- **Rotato**: 3D device mockups (paid)
- **Previewed**: App Store screenshot templates

## ASO Keywords Strategy

### Primary Keywords (High Priority)

| Keyword | Search Volume | Competition | Relevance |
|---------|---------------|-------------|-----------|
| tutor app | High | Medium | Primary |
| tutoring management | Medium | Low | Primary |
| lesson scheduling | Medium | Medium | Primary |
| student management | Medium | Low | Primary |
| private tutor | High | High | Primary |

### Secondary Keywords (Medium Priority)

| Keyword | Search Volume | Competition | Relevance |
|---------|---------------|-------------|-----------|
| teaching app | High | High | Secondary |
| education management | Medium | Medium | Secondary |
| invoice app | High | High | Secondary |
| progress tracking | Medium | Low | Secondary |
| lesson planner | Medium | Medium | Secondary |

### Long-tail Keywords (Lower competition)

- "app for private tutors"
- "tutoring business management"
- "music teacher scheduling"
- "math tutor app"
- "student progress tracker"
- "tutoring invoice generator"
- "lesson booking app"
- "parent communication app for teachers"

### Keyword Placement Strategy

1. **App Title**: Include "Tutoring" (most weight)
2. **Subtitle/Short Description**: Include "tutor", "lessons", "scheduling"
3. **Description First Paragraph**: Use primary keywords naturally
4. **Throughout Description**: Sprinkle secondary and long-tail keywords
5. **iOS Keywords Field**: Focus on words NOT in title/subtitle

### iOS Keyword String Recommendation
```
tutoring,tutor,lessons,students,scheduling,education,teaching,invoice,progress,private lessons
```

Note: Don't repeat words from your title ("Love2Learn", "Tutoring") in the keyword field.

### Localization Opportunities

Consider localizing for:
- UK English (different spelling: "organisation", "colour")
- Spanish (large market for tutoring apps)
- French
- German

## Asset Creation Checklist

### Pre-Submission Checklist

#### Icons
- [ ] iOS App Store icon (1024 x 1024 px, no transparency)
- [ ] Android Play Store icon (512 x 512 px)
- [ ] Android adaptive icon foreground (432 x 432 px)
- [ ] Android adaptive icon background color/image
- [ ] Favicon for web (multiple sizes or 180 x 180 px)

#### Screenshots - iOS
- [ ] iPhone 6.9" screenshots (minimum 3)
- [ ] iPhone 6.7" screenshots (minimum 3)
- [ ] iPhone 6.5" screenshots (optional, can use 6.7")
- [ ] iPhone 5.5" screenshots (optional, can use 6.7")
- [ ] iPad Pro 12.9" screenshots (if supporting tablet)

#### Screenshots - Android
- [ ] Phone screenshots (minimum 2, recommended 4-8)
- [ ] 7" tablet screenshots (if supporting tablets)
- [ ] 10" tablet screenshots (if supporting tablets)

#### Feature Graphic - Android
- [ ] Feature graphic (1024 x 500 px)

#### Videos (Optional)
- [ ] iOS App Preview video
- [ ] YouTube promotional video for Play Store

#### Text Content
- [ ] App name (30 chars)
- [ ] Subtitle (iOS) / Short description (Android)
- [ ] Full description (4000 chars)
- [ ] Keywords (iOS only, 100 chars)
- [ ] Promotional text (iOS, 170 chars)
- [ ] What's New text for updates

#### Legal & Support
- [ ] Privacy Policy URL (required)
- [ ] Terms of Service URL (recommended)
- [ ] Support URL/email (required)
- [ ] Marketing website (recommended)

#### Store Configuration
- [ ] Primary category selected
- [ ] Secondary category selected (optional)
- [ ] Age rating questionnaire completed
- [ ] Data safety/privacy form completed
- [ ] Demo account credentials for reviewers
